Welcome To Alaso Restaurant.

Located in Kenya's capital, Alaso restaurant is a purveyor of fine African dining from the esteemed Chef Abdi.

A collaboration between chef Abdi and Palaso, Alaso is where Nairobi returns to African traditional Eatery. Styled like the saloons of fa'ree, here you can sip wine and enjoy the robust taste of farm-to-table Kenyan fare without the frills.
